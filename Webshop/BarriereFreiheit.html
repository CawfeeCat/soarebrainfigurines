Accessibility

This accessibility statement applies to: https://www.soarebrainfigurines.com

1. Commitment

Soare Brain is committed to making its website accessible and usable for as many people as possible, including users with disabilities

We aim to continuously improve accessibility and follow recognized accessibility principles.

2. Accessibility Status

This website is currently partially compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level A and Level AA.

3. Known Accessibility Limitations

Some areas may not yet fully meet accessibility standards, including:

  • Certain interactive elements may have limited keyboard support
  • Some visual elements may require further contrast optimization
  • Some visual or downloadable materials may not be fully optimized for assistive technologies

4. Improvement Process

Soare Brain is operated by a small team of four private individuals.

We continuously review and improve accessibility where technically and practically possible

5. Feedback

If you experience accessibility issues or require information in an alternative format, please contact us:

  • By using our contact form
  • Or by sending us an Email to: sendhelptoour@soarebrain.com

We will review accessibility concerns and try to provide suitable assistance
