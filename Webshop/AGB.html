Approved returns will be refunded after the returned product has been received and inspected.

Return shipping costs may be the responsibility of the customer unless otherwise required by applicable law.

2. Custom Orders

Custom-made products are created specifically according to customer requirements and may require additional time, resources, and preparation.

Because of their personalized nature, custom orders may only be returned within 14 days after delivery and only under specific circumstances, including:
