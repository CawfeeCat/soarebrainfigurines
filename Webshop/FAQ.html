Frequently asked questions

What are your figurines made of?

Resin, resin and resin! The best material to make figurines. It also has a coating of something very spicy, to prevent people from putting the figurines in openings, which they are not supposed to go into.

What payment services do you accept?

Mastercard, VISA, Revolut, and Union Pay!

Will you add other currencies?

They will be added once we figure out how to make the script work!

What is your return and refund policy, especially for custom orders?

You can send a regular order back within 30 days if you got issues with it. Custom orders can only be sent back if there are damages, manufacturing errors, etc. For more information, view our Terms, Licenses and Refunds page.

What does ordering a custom figurine look like?

You simply go to our contact form, fill everything out (for the subject you can put something like "custom order request" and we will understand immediately). You just need to describe what you have in mind (size, character, resin types) and ideally upload some concept art! We will then get in touch with you personally to discuss everything properly.

I want to order a custom figurine, but I don't have any reference material to provide to you

We can draw the concepts for you too, if you ask nicely!

What will you do with my reference material?

We will only use it for your custom order! And once everything is done, and to your liking, we will delete all of it!

Can I order figurines of characters from other fandoms or creators?

You can, but only if you have a written permission from the creators. (You would have to send this permission along with the reference material)

Why is the upload field on the contact page in a different language?

That's because of your browser settings!
